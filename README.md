# Privacy Policy for BuildChecklists

Last updated: February 2026

BuildChecklists (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

## 1. Data Storage

All project data, checklists, expenses, and related content are stored locally on your device.

BuildChecklists does not operate a remote server and does not transmit your project data to external servers.

## 2. Account Registration

The App allows users to create an account within the application.  
Account information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

## 3. Subscriptions

BuildChecklists offers auto-renewable subscriptions (USER and PRO plans).

All subscription purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store.  
The App does not collect or store your payment information.

For more information about how Apple handles payments and data, please refer to:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## 4. Analytics and Tracking

BuildChecklists does not use third-party analytics services.  
The App does not track users across apps or websites.

## 5. Data Sharing

BuildChecklists does not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

## 6. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, deleting the App will remove all stored data from the device.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the App. Updates will be reflected on this page.
