Privacy Policy — BuildChecklists (Google Play)

Last updated: March 2026

BuildChecklists (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Android version of the App distributed through Google Play.

1. Data Storage

Project data, checklists, expenses, photos, and related content are primarily stored locally on your device.

The App does not transmit your project content to external servers unless explicitly stated in future updates.

2. Account and Profile Data

If you create a profile inside the App, profile-related information is stored locally on your device unless otherwise indicated by a future cloud feature.

3. Subscriptions and Billing

BuildChecklists may offer paid subscriptions such as USER and PRO plans.

Subscription purchases on Android are processed securely by Google Play.

The App does not directly collect or store your payment card details.

For more information about how Google handles payments and account data, please refer to Google Play policies and Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Analytics and Diagnostics

The App may use basic diagnostic information, crash logs, or analytics tools in future versions to improve stability and usability.

If such tools are enabled, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not sell your personal data.

The App does not share your construction project content with third parties except where required by law or platform functionality.

6. User Control

You can delete local application data by removing the application or using in-app account/profile deletion features where available.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age required by applicable local law for digital services without parental consent.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App after updates means you accept the revised policy.
